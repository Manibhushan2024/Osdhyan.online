# OSDHYAN Platform User Guide

This guide explains how to use every major section in the student portal and the admin portal.

## Access and Login

1. Open the platform URL and select Student Portal or Admin Portal.
2. Sign in with your email or mobile number and password.
3. If prompted, complete your profile and set your exam preference.

## Home Dashboard

1. Review your daily plan, study time targets, and activity summary.
2. Add daily or weekly tasks and mark them complete as you study.
3. Track accuracy, total tests taken, and time spent from the top stats.

## Courses

1. Open Courses to view your exam and subject structure.
2. Select a subject, chapter, or topic to follow the learning path.
3. Use the course assets before starting tests to build fundamentals.

## Live Classes

1. Open Live Classes to see the schedule and instructor list.
2. Join the listed session at the scheduled time.
3. Use quick links to jump to study materials after class.

## Practice Hub

1. Open Practice to pick your practice mode.
2. Choose Daily Practice for quick drills.
3. Choose Test Series for full-length missions.
4. Choose Previous Year Papers for exam pattern practice.

## Test Series

1. Open Test Series Hub and search for your exam category.
2. Enroll in a series to unlock tests.
3. Start a test, resume an ongoing attempt, or reattempt completed missions.

## Free Quizzes

1. Open Free Quizzes to attempt short tests without enrollment.
2. Use them for daily accuracy checks or topic refreshers.
3. Review results to plan your next practice block.

## Previous Year Papers

1. Open Prev. Year Papers and select the exam year.
2. Attempt the paper like a real mock test.
3. Review the result and improve time strategy.

## Test Player

1. Read instructions and accept the declaration.
2. Choose your default language and start the mission.
3. Use the question palette to navigate and mark for review.
4. Save answers and submit when ready.

## Results and Performance

1. Open the Mission Debrief after submission.
2. Review total score, accuracy, time taken, and sectional stats.
3. Download or share the report if needed.

## Solutions and AI Assistant

1. Open Detailed Solutions from the result page.
2. Filter by correct, wrong, or skipped questions.
3. Use the AI assistant chat to ask about concepts, mistakes, or shortcuts.

## Syllabus Explorer

1. Open Syllabus to view subjects, chapters, and topics.
2. Expand a subject to see chapter breakdowns.
3. Use this view to plan daily or weekly coverage.

## Notes

1. Open Notes to access personal or shared notes.
2. Use them for quick revision before tests.
3. Update notes after analyzing mistakes.

## Materials

1. Open Materials to access PDFs, images, and resources.
2. Filter by type or search by topic name.
3. Open a material and track progress.

## Blogs

1. Open Blogs for strategy articles and updates.
2. Read short summaries before starting a new topic.
3. Use blogs to plan weekly revision.

## Focus Terminal

1. Open Focus Terminal to start a focus session.
2. Choose the session duration and link it to a study goal.
3. Pause, resume, or stop the session when finished.

## Growth Lab

1. Open Growth Lab to set or update study goals.
2. Track hours spent and remaining targets.
3. Use milestones to stay on schedule.

## Mission History

1. Open Mission History to review past attempts.
2. Revisit old tests to check improvement.
3. Use history to plan reattempts.

## Dossier

1. Open Dossier to edit your profile.
2. Update exam preference and contact details.
3. Upload or change your avatar if required.

## Performance

1. Open Performance to view advanced analytics.
2. Identify strengths and weak areas by topic.
3. Use AI recommendations to plan focused practice.

## Settings

1. Open Settings to update account preferences.
2. Manage security, reset password, or update profile data.
3. Save changes and log out when needed.

## Help Hub

1. Open Help Hub for support or guidance.
2. Raise a support ticket if something is not working.
3. Use help articles for common workflows.

## Admin Portal Overview

1. Open the Admin Portal and login with admin credentials.
2. Use the admin dashboard to view platform-wide stats.
3. Move between Test Series, Tests Pool, Question Bank, Courses, and Settings.

## Admin - Test Series Manager

1. Open Admin Test Series to create or manage series.
2. Upload cover images and descriptions in English and Hindi.
3. Publish or unpublish series based on readiness.

## Admin - Tests Pool

1. Open Tests Pool to create a new test.
2. Choose test type, duration, marks, and negative marking.
3. Add questions and publish the test when ready.

## Admin - Question Bank

1. Open Question Bank and choose exam, subject, chapter, topic.
2. Add bilingual questions and options.
3. Set the correct answer and add explanations.

## Admin - Courses and Materials

1. Open Courses to organize subject and chapter structure.
2. Upload materials by topic and type.
3. Verify that content is visible on the student side.

## Admin - Settings and Analytics

1. Open Settings to manage users and permissions.
2. Check analytics to monitor usage and performance.
3. Use the data to improve content quality.
