# OSDHYAN Full Platform Tutorial

## Purpose

Create a complete, section-by-section walkthrough video that explains how to use the student and admin portal. The video must be clear, structured, and useful for first-time users.

## Format

- Resolution: 1920x1080
- FPS: 30
- Estimated duration: about 95 seconds

## Scene Outline

1. Welcome and overview
2. Login and profile setup
3. Home dashboard and planner
4. Courses and learning
5. Live classes console
6. Practice hub
7. Test series hub
8. Test player
9. Results and performance
10. Solutions and AI assistant
11. Academic tools
12. Productivity stack
13. Identity and settings
14. Admin and institute control
15. Closing message

## Voiceover Script

1. Welcome to OSDHYAN, the complete preparation ecosystem for serious aspirants.
2. We will walk through every section so you can use the platform confidently from day one.
3. Start with the home dashboard, where your daily goals and progress live.
4. Use Courses and Live Classes to build concepts before practice.
5. Move to the Practice Hub, Test Series, and PYQs to simulate real exam pressure.
6. Take tests in bilingual mode, manage attempts, and submit with full control.
7. Review results, accuracy charts, and sectional insights after every mission.
8. Use the Solutions hub and AI assistant to learn from every mistake.
9. Stay organized with syllabus, notes, and study materials.
10. Keep momentum with Focus Terminal and Growth Lab.
11. Update your profile and settings inside Dossier and Performance.
12. For coaching teams, the admin portal controls question banks, test creation, and content operations.
13. OSDHYAN turns preparation into a repeatable, results-driven system.
